It has been a long time since we've seen each other. The last time was at an airport where you worked a long while ago. I thought about you recently a month ago. 

===

I hope you (the reader) allow me to be a bit selfish and express my thoughts in the best way I know.

Admittedly, I've been avoiding thinking of it and I've actively burried myself in work for the last two weeks. This week was different. I took some time off work this week to see you, even if it was only for a few minutes. It was the first time I've seen you since the airport 10+ years ago. I'm ashamed to say it's been that long.

The stories people have shared in the last few days talked about a good person who's life was almost dedicated to connecting people. That is what you did for me. I was that awkward kid 20 years ago, a goof! In a lot of ways I'm still _that_ kid. You always had a friends around, and the girls all wanted to be around you. To be honest in the beginning, I didn't really think we had a lot in common. You proved to me that it didn't matter much. It seems my story wasn't that special; this is what you did. You befrend, you connect and you lift people up. That is what you did for me. Through you, I became connected with more. I saw and learned what it meant to be friendly, honest and thoughtful.

I remember we would have long chats while ~~we~~ you played guitar and I watched. Your deep thoughts that came out as wisdom and your mature outlook were things that many admired about you. However, for me it was just simply your friendship. Maybe it was the fact that you were older, maybe it was the amount of people who hung on your words, or maybe it was just you. 

I'll miss you man.
